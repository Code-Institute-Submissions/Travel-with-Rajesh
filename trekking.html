Everest Region

Gallery
Everst Base Camp*
  • Duration: 14 days
  • Max Elevation: 5364 m
  • Difficulty: Medium to Hard
  • Accessible: For everyone how likes to challange them selfs and enjoys an adventurous walk through a scenery of the mountain
  • During: This 14 day trek will offer a panoramic view of the highste mountains, you will encounter Sherpa Culture and tibetan Buddhism. Sagarmatha National Park, is the highest national park in the world. Sagarmāthā is the Nepali name for Everest and it means goddess of the sky. Trekking in the Sagarmatha National Park offers very majestic view of beautiful mountain like Mt. Everest ( 8.848 m), the Ama Dablam ( 6865m), Lhotse (8.516 m), Nuptse (7.861 m). The trail leads through beautiful villages, monasteries and you will see the friendly local people of the Khumbu region. Walking through a beautiful pine forest, the track leads us along the Dudh Koshi River over many suspension bridges, one of which is the famous Hillary Suspension Bridge. You will trek towards Tengboche while enjoying the views of Ama Dablam and close up view of Thamserku, the gradual trail will have some ups and downs. You will catch the views from Kala Patthar and you will witness the first light of day shining on Mt. Everest. During the ascent to Kala Patthar you will have a beautiful view of the Mahalangur himalaya range.

Annapurna Region

Gallery
Annapurna Base Camp*
  • Duration: 10-12 days
  • Max Elevation: 4130 m
  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Accessible: For everyone who enjoys an adventurous walk through a scenery of the mountain.
  • During: This 10 to 12 days trekking will take you through rice fields, waterfalls and rivers, the rhododendron Forest and typical Nepali villages with the beautiful view of Annapurna Himalaya Range. The path will lead you to a beautiful village called Ghorepani (2810m). This village belongs to one of the ethinic group call Magar and from here you will have an impressive view of the rhododendron forest and the Himalaya. It is worth the climb to Poon Hill in the early morning for a panoramic view of the sunrise and 14 different mountains. Another village you will travel to is Chhomrong and it is a beautiful Gurung village which lies in the lap of Annapurna South, with views of many moutains including Mt. Himal Chuli and Mt. Fish Tail. The final destination of this Trek is Annapurna Base Camp and it is surrounded by the Annapurna Himalayan Range with the altitude of 4130m. Jhinu Danda is a good place to let go of all your tiredness of walking with a hot spring. The trail will lead us back to Nayapul along the river, called Madi, which comes all the way from Annapurna South.
Annapurna Round*
  • Duration: 17 days
  • Max Elevation: 5416 m
  • Difficulty: Medium to Hard
  • Accessible: For everyone how likes to challange them selfs and enjoys an adventurous walk through a scenery of the mountain
  • During: this 17 days trek,The Annapurna Circuit is the most popular trek in Nepal. Like all the best journey's, the trek reveals itself gradually, as it climbs through subtropical jungle to a Tibetan-influenced valley and then over the high Thorung La (5416m) to the Kali Gandaki Valley, a desert-like Trans- Himalayan region that was once a vital trade corridor to Tibet. The real appeal of this trek lies in it's variety. The trail winds through rice paddies and climbs through the deep gorge of the Marsyangdi, before swinging West into the arid Manang region, with its awesome views of the North side of the Annapurnas.

Ghorepani Poon Hill*
  • Duration: 12 days
  • Max Elevation: 3210 m
  • Difficulty: Easy to Medium
  • Accessible: For everyone who enjoys an adventurous walk through the scenery of mountains.
  • During: This 5 day trek will take you to rice fields, a waterfall and river, the rhododendron forest and to typical Nepali villages with a beautiful views of the Annapurna Himalaya Range. The trail will lead you to the beautiful village called Ghorepani (2810 m). This village belongs to one of the ethnic groups called Magar. From here you will have an impressive view of the rhododendron forest and the Himalaya. Poon Hill (3210 m) will be the highest point of your trek. From where you will have a beautiful panoramic view of the sunrise with 18 different mountains including Annapurna I (8091 m), Fishtail (6993 m) and Mount Dhaulagiri (8167 m) in the background. In the lap of the Annapurna mountain range, you will visit typical Gurung villages called Tadapani and Ghandruk. Here, you will have a wonderful view of Mt. Fishtail and Annapurna South. On the way down to Nayapul, you will walk through the peaceful landscape of rice fields and a waterfall.

Gallery
Langtang Valley*
  • Duration: 7-9 days
  • Max Elevation: 3850 m
  • Difficulty: Easy to Medium
  • Accessible: For everyone who enjoys an adventurous walk through the scenery of mountains.
  • During: This 7 to 9 days trekking in the Langtang valley offers a small view of the Tibetan culture and rituals, as well as a view of different landscapes. You get to see the rhododendrons forest Valleys, rivers, waterfalls and Glacier at a low comfortable elevation. Furthermore, there are many mountains that you get to see along the trail. For example Mt. Langtang Lirung (7246m.), Mt. Gang Chhenpo (6388m), Mt. Naya Gang (5846m) and Mt. Dorje Lakpa( 6966m). The trail goes through the rhododendron forest alongside the Langtang river that flows to Mt. Kyanjing gompa (3850m). You will visit a Buddhist monastery and the oldest yak cheese factory of the world. It is worth spending one extra day in Kyanjing gompa to hike some of the following trails the Kyanjing RI, the Cherko RI or the Lang SE SA RI kharka. The trail will go back the same way to Shyapru Bensi or Dhunche.

Manaslu Round*
  • Duration: 14 to 16 days
  • Max Elevation: 5106 m
  • Difficulty: Medium to Hard
  • Accessible:
  • During: This 15 to 16 day trek will guide you through some beautiful Gurung villages and the Tibetan culture,it will bring you to the peaceful Budhi Gandaki valley, which is located in the Gorkha region. This is the land from where the first king of Nepal started to creating Nepal. You can enjoy the relaxing sounds of the river while you pass local people working on the rice fields. futher you will see green hills, waterfalls and a hot spring. While walking through the forest you will see wildlife like birds, deer, monkeys and the Himalayan blue sheep. During this hike you will get to know the typical way of Nepali life with different ethnic groups. The trail goes through SamaGaun, where you take one extra day to walk up to ManasluBasecamp (4500 m). Mount Manaslu is also known as the mountain of Spirit. From this place you can visit the Glacier Lake. You continue the path through Samdo to the highest point of our hike, Larke La (5106 m). To get down to BesiSahar, you will walk on the Annapurna Circuit trail for a few days.
  • minimum with 2 persons because it's a restricted area (special permit needed)
