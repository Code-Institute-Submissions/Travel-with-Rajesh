Chitwan

  • Duration: 10-12 days
  • Max Elevation: 4130 m
  • During: this 10 to 12 days walk, you will cross the rice field, the waterfall and river, the rhododendron forest and a typical Nepali village with the beautiful view of the Annapurna Himalaya Range.
  • Accessible: for everyone who enjoys an adventurous walk through the scenery of the mountain. It is worth climbing to Poon Hill in the early morning for a panoramic view of the sunrise and 14 different mountains. Chhomrong is a beautiful Gurung village, that is situated in the lap of Annapurna South.
